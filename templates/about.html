About the project

Pro triathlon, decoded.

Pro Tri Data tracks over 60k athletes and 9k races across every level of World Triathlon racing. We combine results, ratings, and context to make performance and development easier to explore.

What you'll find here

  • Career summaries for every athlete.
  • Race pages with splits, ratings, and performance analysis.
  • Head-to-head comparisons between any athletes across eras.
